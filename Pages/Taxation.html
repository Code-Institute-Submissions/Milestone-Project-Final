Taxation

Taxation Tabel

The taxation tabel dermines how much tax should be deducted from your general income. You can not choose your taxation tabel. The payroll department automaticaly uppdates your taxation tabel in the beging of each year.


Tax on Extra Income

Tax on extra income is 30%. This tax is applied to bonuses and to all income from extra or ocasional work.


Volontary Extra Tax

You have the right to demand that we deduct an extra sum in tax. Mail the payroll department how much extra you wish to pay each month and we will take care of it for you.


Tax Adjustement

If you wish to pay less in tax then determined by your taxation tabel you have to apply for a tax adjustment with the IRS. Send your tax adjustment decision to the payroll department.
