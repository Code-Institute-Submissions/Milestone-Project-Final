Nordic Choice Payroll

Employment Forms

In accordance with current labour laws and our collective bargening agreements Nordic Choice uses the below forms of employment. Your employment form is specified on your contract.


Employed for Indefinite Duration

The employment is ongoing. This is the default employment form. If the employment form have been left unspecified in the contract then the employment is automaticaly ongoing . Full LAS protection aplies.


Probationary Period Employment

The first six months of your employment is a probationary period. Once the probationary period is over you automaticaly become employed for indefinite duration. During the probationary period most LAS protections does not apply. Both you and the employeer may choose to terminate your contract without any notice.


Time Limited Employment

When an employment is time limited this form is used. Seasonal work, temporary positons or project specific employments are sub categories.


Employed for Ocasional or Extra Work

LAS protections does not apply. Unlike the other forms of employment vacation pay is payed out continuasly. Under this form of employement schedualing of shifts may not be done more then 2 weeks ahead. There is no set hours that have to be worked. An employee for ocasional or extra work is only concidered an emplyee of the company for the duration of the shift. Income from such work is taxed at 30%.
