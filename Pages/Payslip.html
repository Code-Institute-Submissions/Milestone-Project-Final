Payslip

Each month you will recive a payslip that specifies what you have earned and what deductions have been made. In order to be GDPR compliant we are not allowed to email payslips.

Instead the payslip is sent to Kivra or other government aproved secure electronic mail box. If you do not have an electronic mail box the payslip will be sent by post to the adress you have specified on your contract. We recomend that you archive your payslips for at least five years.

If you are missing or have lost a payslip ask your manager to print you a new one.
