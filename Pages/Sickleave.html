Sick Leave


The First 14 Days

The first sick day is a waiting period, which means that you will not receive any pay for this day. For sick day 2-14 you will recive sick pay from Nordic Choice. Your sick pay is 80% of the sallery you would noramly have recived for that period. If you are sick seven days or more you have to get a doctors certificate to qualify for sick pay.


Day 15 and Forward

How your sickleave is handled after day 14 depends on what collective bargening agreement you are covered by. If you are a worker you are under HRF rules. Managers and administrators are covered by Unionen.


HRF

From sick day 15 and forward you will have to apply to Försäkringskassan for compensation. No sick pay is payed by the company and a deduction is made for each day of absence.
