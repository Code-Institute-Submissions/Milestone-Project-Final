Taxation

Taxation Table

The taxation table determines how much tax should be deducted from your main income. You canÂ´t choose your taxation table. The payroll department automatically updates your taxation table in the begining of each year.

You may check what taxation table that is applicable for you by using the IRS taxation table finder.


Tax on Extra Income

Tax on extra income is 30%. This tax is applied to bonuses and to all income from extra or occasional work.


Voluntary Extra Tax

You have the right to demand that we deduct an extra sum in tax. Mail the payroll department how much extra you wish to pay each month and we will take care of it for you.


Tax Adjustment

If you wish to pay less in tax then determined by your taxation table you have to apply for a tax adjustment with the IRS. Send your tax adjustment decision to the payroll department. You may apply for a tax adjustment here.
