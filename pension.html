Pension

The payroll department will sign you up with the Collectum pension plan and repport your earnings. Please note that if you work extra your income does not generate pension benefits. We at the payroll department only report your earnings to Collectum. Thus, we are only able to answer questions concerning what we have reported. If you have other questions regarding your pension, we recommend that you contact Collectum directly.


ITP 1 or ITP 2?

What retirement plan you are in depends on when you were born.



For more information about your pension, visit the Collectum web page.
