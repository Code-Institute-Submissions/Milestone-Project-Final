Sick Leave


The First 14 Days

The first sick day is a waiting period, which means that you will not receive any pay for this day. For sick day 2-14, you will recive sick pay from Nordic Choice. Your sick pay is 80% of the salary you would normally have received for that period. If you are sick, seven days or more you have to get a doctors certificate to qualify for sick pay.


Day 15 and Forward

How your sick leave is handled after day 14 depends on what collective bargaining agreement you are covered by. If you are a worker, you are under HRF rules. Managers and administrators are covered by Unionen.


HRF

From sick day 15 and forward, you will have to apply to Försäkringskassan for compensation. No sick pay is payed by the company and a deduction is made for each day of absence.


Unionen

Sick pay of 10% of your normal salary will be payed by Nordic Choice for day 15-90. Försäkringskassan will give you up to 80% of your income in government sick benefits. Thus, you can get up to 90% of your normal income while sick.


Insurance

You may have insurances that entitles you to compensation when you get sick. To calculate the full amount you may get if falling sick please see Erättningskollen.
